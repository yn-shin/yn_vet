- mature lymphocyte가 관찰됨. Lymphoblatic, lymphocyte도 함께 발견됨.

#### Plasma cell inclusion

- Mott cell : 염증
	- Russel body : IgG가 inclusion처럼 관찰되는 것. refractile blue green cytoplasmic inclusion
	- inflammation
- Dutcher body : 염증 & 종양
	- intranuclear inclusion
	- plasmacytoid lymphoma
